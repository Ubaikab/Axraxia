2007 Canadian Million Dollar

The 2007 Canadian Million Dollar Coin, crafted from 99.999% pure gold, is one of the world's largest coins, weighing 100 kg (220 lbs). Featuring a design by artist Susan Taylor, it showcases the iconic Canadian maple leaf. Its immense value and rarity make it a symbol of Canada's wealth and artistry.

Price: $5 million CAD
