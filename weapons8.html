Persian Shamshir

The Persian shamshir is a deeply curved, single-edged sword known for its elegance and precision. Favored by Persian cavalry, it was effective for slashing from horseback. Shamshirs were often elaborately decorated, symbolizing wealth and status, and represented the height of sword craftsmanship in Persia during the Safavid period.
