1913 Liberty Head Nickel

The 1913 Liberty Head Nickel is one of the rarest and most famous coins in American numismatics. Only five known specimens exist, featuring a Liberty head design on the obverse and a modified reverse. Its rarity and historical significance have made it a coveted item among collectors, often fetching millions at auctions.

Price: $8 million
