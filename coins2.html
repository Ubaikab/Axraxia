1933 Saint-Gaudens

The 1933 Saint-Gaudens $20 gold coin is a celebrated piece of American numismatic history, designed by sculptor Augustus Saint-Gaudens. It features a radiant Lady Liberty on the obverse and a majestic eagle on the reverse. Due to its limited mintage and the rarity of surviving examples, it is highly sought after by collectors.

Price: $20,000
