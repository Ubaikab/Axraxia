Cretan Jug [Stamnos]

Cretan jugs, such as the stamnos, were used for storing liquids like wine. Well-preserved examples, particularly those with decorative or mythological motifs, are highly collectible. Intact pieces with clear provenance, especially from important excavation sites, can fetch higher prices at auctions or through private sales.
