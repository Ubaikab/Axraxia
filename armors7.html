Egyptian Pharaoh Armor

Egyptian pharaohs donned ceremonial armor made from gold, bronze, and precious stones. These pieces, while ornate and highly decorative, also provided protection during battles and rituals. The armor symbolized divine kingship, wealth, and power, often featuring symbols like the Ankh or scarab, associated with life and rebirth.
