1909-S VDB Lincoln Cent

The 1909-S V.D.B. Lincoln Cent is a rare and highly coveted coin in American numismatics. Designed by Victor David Brenner, it features a young Abraham Lincoln on the obverse and wheat stalks on the reverse. Only a limited number were minted, making it a prized collectible for enthusiasts and investors alike.

Price: $150,000
