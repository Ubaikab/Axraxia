1907 Relief Saint-Gaudens

The 1907 Relief Saint-Gaudens $20 gold coin, designed by Augustus Saint-Gaudens, is renowned for its stunning artistry and high relief. Featuring a majestic Lady Liberty on the obverse and an eagle in flight on the reverse, it symbolizes American prosperity. Its rarity and beauty make it a prized collectible.
