1916-D Mercury Dime

The 1916-D Mercury Dime is a sought-after coin in American numismatics, featuring a winged Liberty on the obverse and a fasces with an olive branch on the reverse. Minted in Denver, its low production numbers contribute to its rarity, making it highly prized among collectors and investors alike.

Price: $25,000
