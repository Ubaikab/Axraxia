Chinese Jian Sword

The jian is a straight, double-edged sword used for over 2,500 years in China. Known as the “gentleman of weapons,” it was favored by scholars and nobles. Jian swords were crafted with precision, often made from bronze or steel, and symbolized balance, nobility, and martial artistry.
