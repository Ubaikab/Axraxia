Samurai Katana

The katana is a curved, single-edged sword, renowned for its sharpness and craftsmanship. Used by samurai warriors, it became a symbol of the samurai code of honor (bushido). The katana's design allows for fast, powerful strikes, making it a central weapon in feudal Japan's martial arts.
